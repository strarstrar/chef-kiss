Ingriefients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Garlic Clove
  • Salt and Pepper

Starps

  1. In a pot big enough to fit your long noodles on high temperature, fill with water, around 2 pinches of salt, and noodles to boil.
  2. Roll ground beef into any sized balls.
  3. In a pan on medium-high temperature, and with budder or bit of olive oil, add meat balls into the pan and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook meat balls until sufficiently cooked. Keep on medium to low heat to wait for noodles to boil or wait before starting on meat balls.
  5. Chop onion into pieces.
  6. Crush and chop garlic clove.
  7. Strain noodles. Leave some left over water to not dry out the noodles.
  8. Add onion, garlic, and marinara sauce.
  9. Stir and add salt and pepper if needed.
  10. Add noodles to a dish and allocate any number of meat balls to your dish.

Starps

Lasabra Noodles

  1. Whisk 8 eggs in a mixing bowl or flour bowl, adding flour a bit at a time with some salt until dough is more solid. Knead with hands until it's smooth and doughy.
  2. Shape dough into a ball and wrap in plastic wrap in room temperature for one hour.
  3. Divide dough into 4 portions and roll with more flour until long and flat. Use a pasta roller attatchment or divide more portions if there isn't enough room for long pasta. Just enough to fit pasta sheets in the Lasabra pan for 4 layers.
  4. On a baking tray, cover with parchment pape and line pasta sheets on the tray. Add another layer of parchment paper with pasta until all is on the tray.
  5. Wrap the tray with plastic wrap, and set it aside.

Red Sauce

  1. Pull meat out of sausage casings.
  2. Chop onions, crush fennel seeds, and mince 5 cloves of garlic.
  3. In a pot, heat olive oil over medium to high heat until shimmering.
  4. Add sausage allowing it to sear. Break the sausage into pieces, then turn heat to medium.
  5. Add chopped onions with a sprinkle of salt and stir until onion and sausage are browning.
  6. Add minced garlic, crushed fennel seeds, sprinkle of crushed red pepper and black pepper. Stir while grounding the sausage.
  7. Add entire tube of tomato paste, then stir until paste is a darker red.
  8. Add whole peeled tomatos, crush tomatos, and stir to mix.
  9. Add a bunch of basil leaves and leave pot uncovered to cook.
  10. Stir occasionally, add spices if needed, and remove from heat when sauce reduces by 25%.

Ricotta Filling

  1. Add whole milk ricotta to a mixing bowl.
  2. Add coat of shredded parmesan cheese.
  3. Add 3 minced cloves of garlic.
  4. Add black pepper and a pinch of salt.
  5. Add ground nutmeg.
  6. Stir and add spices if needed.
  7. Add 2 eggs and mix until combined.

Assembly

  1. Add thin layer of red sauce to bottom of a lasagna pan.
  2. Add pasta sheets to cover bottom of pan. Trim to fit if needed.
  3. Add even layer of ricotta cheese.
  4. Add light coat of graded mozzarella.
  5. Add a sprinkle of parmesan cheese.
  6. Add a layer of red sauce.
  7. Repeat with adding pasta sheets, keeping layer even and level.
  8. On last layer, add a layer of red sauce, spread scoops of ricotta cheese, a thick layer of mozzarella, and full cover coat of parmesan cheese.
  9. On a baking sheet, bake in oven until top is golden brown.

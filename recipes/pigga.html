Ingriefients

  • Dough
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Pepperoni
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt

Starps

  1. In a bowl, add tomato sauce and salt, then stir.
  2. Flatten dough with a roller or stretch out with hands.
  3. Place stretched out dough on a round pan.
  4. Spread tomato sauce on dough.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella on pigga.
  6. Place pepperoni around the pigga.
  7. On high temperature, place pan in oven and watch it cook until bubbling and browning.

Starps

  1. Portion ground beef into one patty. Portion multiple if you want a multi stack burber.
  2. In a pan with butter on medium-high temperature, add in ground beef patty.
  3. Add optimal salt and pepper to patty.
  4. Cook patty until not raw.
  5. Chop onion and tomato to have slices.
  6. Split brioch buns and place an american cheese on the bottom bun.
  7. Scoop cooked beef patty and place it on top of the cheese covered bottom bun and add another cheese slice on the beef patty. Add another beef patty and slice of cheese on top for a multi stack burber.
  8. Spread mayo, ketchup, and mustard on the top bun respectively.
  9. Add pickles to the top bun.
  10. Break off a leaf of lettuce from the iceberg lettuce and place it on the top bun.
  11. Add tomato and onion slices to the top bun.
  12. Combine both buns.
